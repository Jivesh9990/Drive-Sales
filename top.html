The Drivesales On-boarding Process

Each applicant who we on-board on The DriveSales™ platform must clear the entire onboarding process designed to measure sales expertise, soft skills, and workplace competency. The entire onboarding process between 2-3 weeks to complete.

STEP 1

Sales Competency

We begin our process by testing the applicant sales competency by well-defined and proven sales assessments. The purpose of the assessment is to test applicant sales subject matter expertise, technical dynamics, and problem-solving skill required in sales."

STEP 2

Soft Skill and Workplace Ethics

We ensure every applicant we on-board can express their ideas effectively. For which every applicant goes through various Soft Skill assessments. These help us determine applicant personality traits and work ethics."

STEP 3

Face to Face Interview

After the written assessment, each candidate is personally interviewed by The DriveSales™ subject matter expert for their functional domain. Our experts screen talents for specific live exercises, pro-active & design thinking, and subject matter expertise."

STEP 4

Due Diligence

Upon the assessment and documentation, we request the applicant to submit a relevant recommendation letter from their industry leaders, we subsequently cross-check their professional records and work experience."

STEP 5

Internal Training & Quality Check

Upon the assessment and interview, every applicant is given an internal assignment to ensure the delivery of the magical experience to the team they work with. It’s a brief two-week process where the applicant is checked for work ethics and domain expertise."

STEP 6

Pitch Video

What is better than the sales talent making the pitch to you on why they should be hired and work for you. Every applicant who passes our 5-step process is being asked you record a pitch video not more than 90 seconds stating their sales experience and functional domain knowledge."
